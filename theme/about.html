About Me!

I am a designer that loves creating unique visual design solutions for print, web, and apparel design.

I have experience working as a graphic production artist for J.Jill where I created catalog page layouts and marketing assets in a collaborative environment. In addition, I also created digital, print, and apparel assets for my previous employer. I am a motivated self-starter, that loves to learn and create thought-provoking and engaging designs.

When I am not working I love drawing, being outdoors, hiking, running, enjoying the sunshine and being inspired!
