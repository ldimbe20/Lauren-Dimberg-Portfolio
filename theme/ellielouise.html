Ellie Louise

Ellie Louise is a fictional boutique that I created to showcase my design process and was influenced by my time working at Jade Marketing Group, a denim company. Although I am unable to showcase any of the work I have done for them online, I feel this shows skills I learned while working for the company.

Website and Mobile Design

While creating this website and phone application, I was influenced by information I learned taking a User Interface and User Experience class. My designs are simple, elegant, and user-friendly.

My goal is to create digital platforms that are both visually pleasing and cohesive, but also easy for users to navigate. To accomplish this, I studied other e-commerce sites and documented what features I found cumbersome as well as what I found easy to use. I solicited feedback from my peers on their user experiences for a well-rounded perspective. The end result is aesthetically pleasing and easy-to-use.
