C.R. Gibson

As a designer for the C.R. Gibson baby team I acquired experience researching, updating, and designing new products for their company. This includes creating renders for potential products and also creating certain products from start to finish. Below are some of my favorite creations and some adorable babies!

Stationary and Keepsake Items

Matching Baby Boy and Baby Girl bunny cards to celebrate the arrival of a little one. The illustrations on these cards were hand done in Illustrator.

The Letters to Baby book was conceptualized after researching current trends and getting inspiration from several other baby shower books and memorial items on the market. This book, set up to mimic an envelope, is a keepsake item with prompts on each page for baby shower guest to fill out as a letter to baby. The book, with vibrant colors and patterns, is a creative way to document friend's and family’s wishes to reread in the future.

This custom baby shower guest book was inspired by the current trends of the millennial mom. Trendy pops of neon, turquoise fringe, and a little glitter update the traditional guest book so it is more relatable to the current demographic.

This Busy Mom Planner is a 17 month book developed to keep mom’s organized and motivated with inspirational quotes, sticker pages, and a pocket for storage. This planner inspired by other planners on the market, but suited to the busy mom by researching and interviewing mothers and how they stay organized.

Photo Props and Accessories

The following products were created as a way to capture photo memories and share with friends and family on social media. While creating these products I researched current baby trends and also social media sharing products.

First Year Milestone Blanket.

Although not a photo props, these diaper tags match back to the milestone blanket and will be sold next to each other. These tags feature a die-cut flap with ID paper insert.

First Year Belly stickers. These belly stickers are used to commemorate baby’s age and special milestones.

Similar to the belly stickers, these photo prop cards commemorate baby’s age, but include humorous milestones.
